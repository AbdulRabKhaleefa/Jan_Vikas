    About MGNREGA

    MGNREGA (Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act) is a transformative social security scheme launched in 2006. It guarantees 100 days of wage employment per year to rural households, aiming to enhance livelihood security. The scheme empowers communities, reduces migration, and builds durable assets like roads, ponds, irrigation canals, and rural infrastructure. With over 2.5 lakh Gram Panchayats covered, MGNREGA has become a cornerstone of inclusive development in India. Key impacts include poverty reduction, women's empowerment (with 55% female participation), and environmental benefits through water conservation and afforestation. In 2022-23, it generated over 300 crore person-days of employment, disbursing â‚¹50,000+ crores in wages. Challenges include timely payments and asset quality, but ongoing reforms are addressing these.
